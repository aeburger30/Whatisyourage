Sociological Aging Profile

A multidimensional assessment of how you age structurally, biologically, socially, and psychologically.

1. Chronological Age Structural
The time elapsed since your birth. Used by society for legal milestones (voting, driving, retirement).
2. Biological Age Functional
Rate your own functional fitness (how your body performs).

Imagine sitting on the floor and standing up without using your hands or knees.

Imagine standing on one leg to put on a sock without holding anything.

If someone unexpectedly tossed keys to you, how likely are you to catch them?

3. Social Age Perception & Identity
Social age is defined by how you perceive your own standing in society.
4. Psychological Age Mindset
Your internal age identity and maturity.
